Simple Contact module
------------------------------------
The module offers a simple adminwise contact form. It can also be used as userwise contact form. 	

Help page: /admin/help/simplecontact


The Simple Contact module offer the following functionalities:

- Add Contacts

Two forms for Add Contacts , /admin/simplecontact/edit (Needs 'administer simplecontact' permission) and /simplecontact/add (Needs 'create simplecontact' permission)

- Edit Contacts

/admin/simplecontact/edit/{cid} (Needs 'administer simplecontact' permission)

- Delete Contacts

/admin/simplecontact/delete/{cid} (Needs 'administer simplecontact' permission)

- List Contacts

/admin/simplecontact/list (Needs 'administer simplecontact' permission)


- Export Contacts

/admin/simplecontact/export (Needs 'administer simplecontact' permission)


Note: {cid} will be replaced by a valid unique contact id
